About My Teaching

Holistic yoga that honors tradition and adapts to modern life.

I am a certified yoga teacher focused on holistic wellness — combining classical Hatha, Vinyasa, Pranayama and guided meditation. My classes are suitable for beginners to advanced students, and I design plans to meet individual goals: flexibility, posture correction, stress relief, pregnancy-safe yoga, senior mobility and athletes' conditioning.

  • Approach: Alignment + breath + mindfulness
  • Focus: Safe practice, injury prevention, progressive learning
  • Mode: In-person / Online / Group / Private
